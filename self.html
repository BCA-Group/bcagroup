Breast Self-Exam

1: Look in the Mirror

  • Stand in front of a mirror with your shoulders straight and your arms on your hips.
  • Look for any visible changes in shape, size, or color.

2: Raise Your Arms

  • Raise your arms and look for the same changes.
  • Ensure you check both sides thoroughly.

3: Check for Discharge

  • Look for any signs of fluid coming out of one or both nipples.
  • Check for any discharge that is watery, milky, yellow, or blood-stained.

4: Lie Down to Feel Your Breasts

  • Lie down and use your right hand to feel your left breast and your left hand to feel your right breast.
  • Use a firm, smooth touch with the first few fingers of your hand, keeping the fingers flat and together.
  • Cover the entire breast from top to bottom, side to side.

5: Feel Your Breasts While Standing or Sitting

  • Feel your breasts while you are standing or sitting.
  • Many women find this easiest in the shower when the skin is wet and slippery.
  • Cover your entire breast, using the same hand movements described in step 4.
